Helping Children Build Friendships Through Robotics

Bot Buddies is a platform designed to help children with autism build meaningful friendships through collaborative robot challenges, games, and rewards.


Get Started Learn More

Our Mission

Social interaction can be difficult for many children on the autism spectrum. Bot Buddies encourages teamwork by transforming social interaction into exciting collaborative challenges using programmable robots.

How It Works

Create an account, connect your robot, and complete interactive challenges with another player. Working together earns points that unlock new robot customizations, accessories, and achievements.
